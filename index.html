Science at St. Joseph's

By Ethan Shine and Oskar Jasiewicz

Here at St. Joseph's Secondary School we have three science labs in total. We have two labs indoors and one outside. We do 2 courseworks at the end of the year where we do experiments on 2 of the following: biology, chemistry and physics.


Science is a core subject and every student does it. Our school takes part in many extra ciricular science activities such as, Scifest, young scientist, NUI maynooth, physics and chemistry trips.
